Transportation

Taniti offers several ways to get to the island and travel between its main areas, including public buses, private buses, taxis, rental cars, and bicycles.

Getting to Taniti

Most visitors arrive in Taniti by air. Some also arrive by cruise ship, which docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night each week.

Airport Access

Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. The airport is currently being expanded so that larger jets can land on the island in the future.

Public Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City and operate daily from 5:00 a.m. to 11:00 p.m.

Private Buses & Taxis

Private buses serve the rest of the island. Taxis are also available in Taniti City for visitors who want a direct way to travel.

Rental Cars

Rental cars are available from a local rental agency near the airport.

Bicycles & Walking

Bikes and helmets can be rented from several vendors, and helmets are required by law. Taniti City is fairly flat and walkable, and many visitors explore the Merriton Landing area on foot.
