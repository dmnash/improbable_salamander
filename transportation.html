Transportation

Getting around Taniti is easy and convenient. Whether you prefer public transit, rental vehicles, or guided tours, we offer multiple options to help you explore the island at your own pace.

Car Rentals

Rent a car and explore the island on your own schedule. Multiple rental agencies offer a variety of vehicles from economy to luxury. Valid international driver's license required.

Taxis & Ride Sharing

Convenient taxi services and ride-sharing apps are available throughout the island. Fixed routes and competitive pricing make taxis an affordable way to get around without driving.

Public Transit

Affordable and efficient bus system serving major towns and attractions. Buses run regularly throughout the day and evening. Perfect for budget travelers and those who prefer not to drive.

Scooter & Bicycle Rentals

Rent scooters or bicycles for a fun way to explore coastal areas and local neighborhoods. Great for shorter distances and experiencing the island up close.

Guided Tours

Join organized tours led by knowledgeable local guides. Tours include transportation and cover major attractions, cultural sites, and hidden gems throughout the island.

Water Taxis & Ferries

Travel between islands and coastal areas by water. Regular ferry services connect Taniti with nearby islands, offering scenic routes and unique perspectives of the archipelago.

Plan Your Journey

Check out our activities, find your ideal accommodation, and discover amazing restaurants. Questions about transportation? Contact us anytime.
