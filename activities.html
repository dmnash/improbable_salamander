Things To Do

Taniti offers a mix of outdoor recreation, cultural experiences, and entertainment for visitors of all ages.

Beaches

Taniti features a variety of beaches, including sandy and rocky areas. Visitors can relax, swim, or enjoy the island’s coastal scenery.

Rainforest & Volcano

Much of the island is covered in tropical rainforest, with a small active volcano at its center. Guided tours are available for visitors interested in exploring these natural areas.

Fishing & Boating

Visitors can take part in fishing tours or explore the island by boat. These activities are popular ways to experience Taniti’s surrounding waters.

Snorkeling & Water Activities

Snorkeling and other water-based activities are available in several locations around the island, offering opportunities to explore marine life.

Local History Museum

Taniti’s museum provides insight into the island’s history, culture, and development, offering a more educational experience for visitors.

Entertainment & Nightlife

Visitors can enjoy a variety of entertainment options, including a movie theater, arcade, bowling alley, and nightlife in Taniti City.

Plan Your Visit
