Things To Do

Discover an island paradise filled with adventure, relaxation, and unforgettable experiences. From pristine beaches to cultural attractions, Taniti has something for every traveler.

Beaches & Water Activities

Experience the crystal-clear waters and white sand beaches of Taniti. Enjoy swimming, snorkeling, surfing, or paddleboarding at our world-class beaches. Perfect for families and adventure seekers alike.

Hiking & Nature Trails

Explore the lush tropical landscape through guided nature hikes. Discover exotic wildlife, stunning viewpoints, and hidden waterfalls. Trails range from easy walks to challenging mountain climbs.

Cultural Experiences

Immerse yourself in Tanitian culture by visiting local markets, museums, and festivals. Learn traditional crafts, sample authentic cuisine, and connect with the island's vibrant communities.

Water Sports & Adventures

For thrill-seekers, enjoy kayaking, deep-sea diving, parasailing, and jet skiing. Our experienced guides ensure safe and exciting adventures on the island's beautiful waters.

Scenic Tours & Sightseeing

Take guided tours to see the island's most iconic landmarks and natural wonders. Visit ancient temples, botanical gardens, and viewpoints that offer breathtaking vistas of the surrounding ocean.

Relaxation & Wellness

Unwind with spa treatments, yoga sessions on the beach, or peaceful meditation retreats. Find your zen in tropical settings designed for ultimate relaxation and rejuvenation.

Ready to Explore?

Browse our accommodations, check out our dining options, and learn about getting around the island. For more information, feel free to contact us.
