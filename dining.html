Dining

Taniti offers a diverse culinary scene featuring fresh tropical flavors, fresh seafood, and international cuisine. From casual beachside eateries to upscale fine dining, discover exceptional meals and unforgettable dining experiences.

Fine Dining

Experience elegant cuisine in sophisticated settings. Our fine dining establishments offer locally-sourced ingredients, innovative chef-driven menus, and impeccable service with stunning ocean views.

Local Cuisine

Taste authentic Tanitian flavors at local restaurants serving traditional dishes. Discover family recipes, regional specialties, and the true heart of island culture through its food.

Beachfront Dining

Enjoy fresh seafood and tropical cuisine with your toes in the sand. Watch the sunset over the ocean while savoring expertly prepared fish, shrimp, and island-inspired dishes.

Casual Cafés & Bistros

Perfect for quick bites and relaxed meals, our cafés offer everything from coffee and pastries to sandwiches and light lunch options. Great for families and casual gatherings.

International Cuisine

From Italian to Asian fusion, explore world-class international restaurants. Our chefs blend global techniques with local ingredients for unique and memorable dining experiences.

Nightlife & Bars

After dinner, experience Taniti's vibrant bar scene. Enjoy tropical cocktails, live music, and dancing at venues ranging from relaxed tiki bars to upscale nightclubs.

Plan Your Stay

Looking for a place to stay? Check out our accommodations and explore exciting activities. Need help getting around? Visit our transportation guide.
