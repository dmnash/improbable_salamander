Welcome to Taniti

Taniti is a small tropical island in the Pacific, known for its beaches, rainforest, and active volcano. Visitors can explore natural landscapes, enjoy local attractions, and experience life in Taniti City and Merriton Landing.

  • Relax on sandy and rocky beaches along Yellow Leaf Bay
  • Explore the rainforest or visit the island's active volcano
  • Enjoy activities like snorkeling, fishing tours, zip-lining, and helicopter rides
  • Choose from a range of lodging and dining options across the island

Start planning your visit by exploring things to do, finding places to stay, or reviewing visitor information. Taniti offers a variety of experiences for a short getaway or an extended stay.
