Places To Stay

Taniti offers a variety of accommodations to suit every traveler's needs and budget. From luxury resorts to cozy family-run lodges, find your perfect island retreat and create lasting memories.

Luxury Resorts

Experience ultimate indulgence at our premier all-inclusive resorts. Enjoy world-class amenities, spa treatments, multiple pools, beautiful beaches, and gourmet dining options.

Boutique Hotels

Stay in charming, intimate hotels with personalized service. Each boutique property offers unique character, local décor, and a more authentic Tanitian hospitality experience.

Beach Villas & Bungalows

Rent private beachfront villas and traditional bungalows for a romantic getaway or family vacation. Wake up to ocean views and enjoy the privacy of your own tropical paradise.

Mid-Range Hotels

Comfortable, well-appointed hotels offering excellent value for your money. These properties feature modern amenities, friendly staff, and convenient locations throughout the island.

Guesthouses & B&Bs

Experience warm island hospitality at cozy guesthouses and bed & breakfasts. Perfect for budget-conscious travelers and those seeking a more personal, home-like experience.

Holiday Apartments

Stay in fully-equipped apartments with kitchens and living spaces. Ideal for families or groups wanting to cook their own meals and enjoy longer stays with more independence.

Ready to Book?

Explore things to do during your stay, discover dining options, and learn how to get around. Have questions? Contact us for more information.
