Places To Stay

Taniti offers lodging options for a range of budgets and travel styles, from simple accommodations to larger resort stays. All lodging on the island is regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Hostel

Visitors looking for a lower-cost stay can choose an inexpensive hostel.

Four-Star Resort

Taniti includes one large, four-star resort for travelers seeking a more full-service stay.

Family-Owned Hotels

Many small, family-owned hotels provide additional lodging options across the island.

Bed and Breakfasts

A growing number of bed and breakfasts offer visitors smaller and more personal places to stay.

Inspected Accommodations

All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Options for Different Needs

Whether visitors are looking for a budget stay or a larger resort experience, Taniti offers several lodging choices to support short visits and longer trips.
